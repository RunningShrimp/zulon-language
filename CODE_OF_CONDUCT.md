# ZULON Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Giving credit where due
- Asking for help when needed
- Helping others when you can

### Unacceptable Behavior

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email
  address, without their explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting

## Responsibilities

### Project Maintainers

**Project maintainers are responsible for:**

- Clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior
- Enforcing these standards fairly and consistently
- Responding to any concerns about community members
- Taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any incidents

**Maintainers have the right and responsibility to:**

- Remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other
  contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for other behaviors that they
  deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful

### Contributors

**Contributors are responsible for:**

- Following this Code of Conduct in all interactions
- Reporting inappropriate behavior to maintainers
- Being respectful and professional in all communications
- Focusing on constructive feedback

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when
an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.
Examples of representing our community include using an official e-mail address,
posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed
representative at an online or offline event.

### Community Spaces

Community spaces include but are not limited to:

- GitHub repositories (issues, PRs, discussions)
- Official Discord channels
- Official forums or mailing lists
- Official social media accounts
- Conferences and events

## Enforcement

### Reporting Incidents

**If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior**, please report it to the
project maintainers.

**How to Report:**

1. **Email**: zulon-conduct@example.com
2. **GitHub Issue**: Create an issue with the "CONDUCT" label
3. **Direct Message**: Contact any maintainer directly

**What to Include:**

- Your contact information (so we can follow up)
- The nature of the incident
- Description of the behavior
- Time and location (if applicable)
- Any evidence (screenshots, logs, etc.)
- Names of those involved (if known)

### Confidentiality

All reports will be kept confidential to the extent possible. We will protect
the privacy of those involved. Information will only be shared with those who
need to know to handle the investigation.

### Investigation and Resolution

**Process:**

1. **Acknowledge**: We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 24 hours
2. **Investigate**: We will investigate the incident thoroughly
3. **Respond**: We will respond to the reporter within 7 days with action taken
4. **Follow Up**: We will follow up on any consequences and monitor for recurrence

**Possible Consequences:**

- Warning
- Temporary or permanent ban from community spaces
- Removal of contributions
- Reporting to appropriate authorities (if necessary)

**The consequences will be:**

- Proportional to the severity of the violation
- Determined by the context of the incident
- Applied consistently regardless of the status of the individuals involved
